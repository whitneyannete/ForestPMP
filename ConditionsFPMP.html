FOREST PRODUCE MOVEMENT PERMIT CONDITIONS

1. Only the original copy of the permit is valid for movement of forest produce
specified..

2. This permit is valid only for a single journey- and permitted forest produce
specified in (3) above

3. This permit can only be issued by a Forest Officer in charge of respective
county or his/her authorized officer

4. This permit does not apply to small quantities (e.g a head-load of FUELWOOD
and 3 bags of charcoal) of forest produce for domestic use.

5. This permit is not applicable to forest produce purchased from a licensed
saw miller and accompanied by an official delivery note.

6. Forest produce from gazetted Forest Reserve must have a Revenue Hammer
Mark where applicable.

7. Officer supervising removal of forest produce must sign original and
duplicate copies of permit to signify removal of produce specified in (3).

8. Transport of forest produce must be done during day-time hours (from 6:00
a.m to 6:00 p.m).

9. The charges for movement of the forest produce will be determined as per
the Forest Service General Order (FSGO)
